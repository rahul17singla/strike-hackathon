About Strike

Strike is an innovative learning platform focused on making computer science education accessible, interactive, and impactful for everyone. Our goal is to empower learners of all backgrounds to become confident developers and problem-solvers through guided learning and practical experience. We provide a wide range of courses in web development, data structures, algorithms, and GenAI — all crafted by industry experts. Every module is designed to bridge the gap between theory and real-world application, ensuring you not only learn concepts but also build meaningful projects. At Strike, learning is more than just watching tutorials. You'll engage in hands-on coding sessions, collaborate with peers, and mentorship from top professionals. We believe in learning by building, and that's why our learners graduate with a strong portfolio that stands out. Our mission is simple — to make tech education inspiring, inclusive, and future-ready. Whether you're a beginner exploring coding for the first time or a professional looking to upskill, Strike provides the tools, community, and confidence you need to grow.
